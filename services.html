Pacific West Tree Company

Services

Pacific West Tree Company offers a wide variety of services to assist you. Here are just a few of the services we provide:

Tree Moving/Transplanting

We can temporarily move trees during construction and transplant them back to give you more mature growth. Tree moving also saves you dollars otherwise spent on new trees.

Tree Spading

The most ideal method to transplant large trees is with our tree spades. Not only is this more economical, but tree spading allows us to handle trees up to 12" in trunk diameter.

Tree Boxing

For larger trees or trees which cannot be accessed by our spade truck, tree boxing is used.

Locating Trees

We are always on the lookout for good trees. If you have trees that need to be removed, we can salvage them as live trees.

Tree Inventory

Our inventory of exceptional quality trees is constantly growing. If we don't have a tree you're looking for in our expanding inventory, we will locate it for you.

We Buy Palms Throughout The State Of California
